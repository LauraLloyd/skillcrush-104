Hi my name is Laura Lloyd and I have some advice for you!

Accept that you'll be out of your depth

It's a horrible feeling, right? Being at sea.

You're following instructions, but you're not actually sure what or why you're doing things, or if you'll remember them later.

But this is quite deep water. It's hard to dip your toe in. You just get wet, feel out of your depth, and flail around.

There is no not-getting-wet way to learn to swim.

I think I haven't had that 'swimming feeling' yet. I'm still kicking around in armbands. But I've come to trust that.
